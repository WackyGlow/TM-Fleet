üö¢ About the Project

SpyFleet is a sophisticated maritime tracking system developed to provide real-time visibility of vessel movements within Esbjerg Harbour and the surrounding Danish coastal waters. The system leverages AIS (Automatic Identification System) technology to monitor and display ship positions, routes, and operational data.

Built specifically for the needs of ship management operations in one of Denmark's busiest ports, SpyFleet offers both general maritime surveillance and specialized tracking capabilities for managed vessels, providing enhanced oversight and operational awareness.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-time AIS message reception and processing
  • Interactive map visualization of vessel positions
  • Customizable ship tracking and watchlists
  • Detailed vessel information and specifications
  • Historical position tracking and route analysis
  • Special highlighting for managed vessels
  • Professional maritime database management
